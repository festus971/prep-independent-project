About Us

Kimaiyos1Electronic is the No.1 Electronics retail outlet in Kenya with world class service centers and aftersales service support.
We have 29 Electronics retail shops across the capitals and key regions in central and Eastern Kenya.
Our online shop can be accessed from anywhere in the region through a secured and highly protected eCommerce shop/portal. Kimaiyos1Electronic is the retail arm for Electronics for the Trade and Distribution Division of the Group.
Our Promise is to provide shoppers and consumers with an opportunity to have access to their Electronic appliance needs at their doorstep.
We provide an array of products and services that cuts across Consumer Electronics, Home Appliances, Small and Domestic Appliances.
Our World Class brand portfolio includes but not limited LG, Samsung, Panasonic, Haier, TCL, Hisense, Philips, Bruhm, Orient, Kaiser, Midea, Scanfrost, Nasco, Syinix, Binatone and many more.
